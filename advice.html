Top FAQs

HOW QUICKLY WILL I GET MY REFUND?

A typical refund claim can take anything from 8-12 weeks to pay out. This time scale is almost entirely dependent on the workload HMRC are under at the time of applying. If we can gather all the needed information from our clients and put together a really comprehensive tax refund claim, we can usually improve on this time scale.  As soon as you're happy to go forward, we'll send your claim to HMRC for approval.  In short, thetaxman needs time to check your details and confirm your refund total, so the sooner you get us the information we need the sooner you'll have your money.

The best way to speed up your ITR Tax Refund is to get your Authorising your agent (64-8) form back to us fast.  This is the form that lets us tackle the taxman on your behalf.  Without 2 physical copies of your 64-8 document, HMRC won't even speak to us about your claim.  It's annoying, but it's all about protecting you.

As soon as your refund's paid out, we'll either send you a cheque or pay it into your bank account if you prefer.  The choice is yours.

Remember that you can claim for the last 4 tax years and you can make your claim at any time.

AM I DUE A TAX REBATE

In HMRC's own words, a tax rebate is "a refund on taxes when the tax liability is less than the taxes paid". What it definitely is not is a prize or a dodgy way of ''cheating the system''. Quite simply, when you're owed a HMRC tax refund, it's because you've already paid too much tax.
