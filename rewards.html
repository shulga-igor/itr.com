How ITR rewards work

Join an exclusive community and gain access to some of the UKs best deals, offers and savings.

Lets be honest, sorting through your finances isn’t the most exciting task of the day. That’s why here at ITR we offer little pick-me-ups to help boost your mood.

Register your income tax refund with ITR and get selected discounts.

You’ll be eligible for all the ITR partner rewards as long as you have registered you claim with us. You can earn rewards like a weekly coffee, monthly cinema tickets and more.

How do I get rewards?

  • Step 1

    To become an ITR member, simply use our refund calculator and register with us.

  • Step 2

    Ensure the details you give us are accurate so that we can stat the process of claiming your refund.

  • Step 3

    Check your email, sign our terms and we will open up our rewards – simple!
