Who is eligible?

To benefit from Pension Tax Refund:

  • You must have withdrawn from your pension starting at 55 years or above

To benefit from Pension Tax Refund:

  • You must have withdrawn from your pension starting at 55 years or above
  • Must have taken a Lump Sum, Drawdown or purchase an Annuity.
  • Have the first 25% of your entire pension savings be tax-free.

To benefit from Marriage Allowance:

  • You must be married or in a civil partnership
  • You do not pay Income Tax or income is below Personal Allowance (roughly around £12,500)
  • Your partner pays Income Tax at the basic rate, with an income between £12,501 and £50,000 before receiving Marriage Allowance

*Marriage Allowance is not allotted to you if you are living together, but not married or in a civil partnership.
